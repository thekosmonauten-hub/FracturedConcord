## üöÄ **How to Use the Stat Template**

### **Step 1: Access the Template Editor**
1. **Open the editor** (Tools ‚Üí Passive Tree ‚Üí JSON Data Editor)
2. **Go to "Bulk Operations" tab**
3. **Click "Edit Stat Template"** to expand the template editor
4. **See all stat categories** organized like the individual stat editor

### **Step 2: Customize Your Template**
1. **Set the values** you want for each stat
2. **Use the scroll view** to access all stat categories:
   - Core Attributes (Strength, Dexterity, Intelligence)
   - Combat Resources (Health, Energy Shield, Mana, Reliance)
   - Combat Stats (Attack Power, Defense, Critical Chance, etc.)
   - Damage Modifiers (Increased Physical, Fire, Cold, etc.)
   - Added Damage (Added Physical, Fire, Cold, etc.)
   - Elemental Conversions (Physical as Fire, etc.)
   - Resistances (Physical, Fire, Cold, Lightning, Chaos, etc.)
   - Defense Stats (Armour, Evasion, Energy Shield, etc.)
   - Recovery Stats (Life Regen, Mana Regen, Leech, etc.)
   - Combat Mechanics (Attack Speed, Cast Speed, Movement Speed, etc.)
   - Card System Stats (Cards Drawn, Hand Size, etc.)
   - Legacy Stats (Armor Increase, Spell Power, etc.)

### **Step 3: Apply the Template**
1. **Enable "Bulk Mode"** in the Bulk Operations tab
2. **Select cells** in the Cell Browser or Grid View tab
3. **Return to Bulk Operations** tab
4. **Click "Apply Stat Template"** to apply your custom template

## üõ†Ô∏è **Template Actions**

### **Reset Template**
- **Click "Reset Template"** to clear all template values
- **Starts fresh** with all stats at zero
- **Useful** when you want to create a new template from scratch

### **Load from Selected Cell**
- **Select a cell** in the Cell Browser or Grid View
- **Click "Load from Selected Cell"** to copy its stats to the template
- **Useful** when you want to base a template on an existing cell
- **Requires** a cell to be selected first

### **Apply Stat Template**
- **Select multiple cells** in bulk mode
- **Click "Apply Stat Template"** to apply your custom template
- **All selected cells** will get the template stats
- **Changes are saved** to prefabs automatically

## üéØ **Best Practices**

### **For Template Creation**
- **Start with a base template** (e.g., all zeros)
- **Set only the stats** you want to apply
- **Leave unused stats** at zero
- **Test with one cell** before applying to many

### **For Template Application**
- **Select cells carefully** to avoid overwriting important stats
- **Use Grid View** to see which cells you're selecting
- **Check the console** for confirmation messages
- **Verify changes** by opening the prefabs

### **For Template Management**
- **Save your templates** by noting the values
- **Create different templates** for different node types
- **Use "Load from Selected Cell"** to copy existing good stats
- **Reset templates** when starting new projects
