Tempo Cards
“I act faster than the enemy expects.”

Core Fantasy
Short-term acceleration, momentum, turn manipulation.

Mechanical Identity
Energy gain
Cost reduction
Extra actions
Momentum generation
Agitate generation
Flow generation
Card draw now, not later

Typical Traits
Low to medium base damage
Strong turn-based advantages
Often conditional

Example Effects
Gain Momentum
Reduce next card cost
Draw cards this turn
Trigger extra hits


Burst Cards

“Everything converges into one devastating moment.”

Core Fantasy
Delayed setup → massive payoff.

Mechanical Identity
Spend resources 
- Momentum
- Preparation 
- Discard
- Tolerance, Agitate, Potential Charges
- Flow 
- Corruption 
- Crumble
- Status Effects
Big AoE or single-target hits
Often ends the turn or combat

Typical Traits
High cost or delayed effect
Punishing if mistimed
Extremely rewarding if planned

Example Effects
Spend all Momentum
Consume all Preparation
Trigger delayed explosions


Engine Cards
“If I keep this running, I win.”

Core Fantasy
Repeatable value, snowballing advantage.

Mechanical Identity
Persistent effects
Scaling per action
Triggers on play / discard / hit

Typical Traits
Low immediate impact
High long-term value
Often Powers or Skills

Example Effects
“Whenever you gain X…”
Stack accumulation
Resource loops


Control Cards
“You don’t get to play your game.”

Core Fantasy
Restricting enemies, forcing bad turns.

Mechanical Identity
Stun, Stagger, Bind, Chill
Card denial
Turn disruption

Typical Traits
Lower damage
High tactical value
Strong against elites/bosses

Example Effects
Reduce card draw
Delay enemy actions
Disable defenses

Attrition Cards
“I don’t need to kill you quickly — just inevitably.”
Core Fantasy
Slow, crushing pressure through damage over time.

Mechanical Identity
Ailments
Scaling debuffs
Spread mechanics

Typical Traits
Lower upfront damage
Strong against large packs
Requires patience

Example Effects
Poison, Ignite, Wither
Stack amplification
Damage conversion

Defense / Sustain Cards
“You will not break me.”
Core Fantasy
Survivability with counterplay.

Mechanical Identity
Guard
Regeneration
Damage reduction
On-hit retaliation

Typical Traits
Reactive effects
Defensive scaling
Often synergize with control

Example Effects
Guard refresh
Damage reflection
Healing over time

Utility / Deck Manipulation
“I reshape my deck mid-combat.”
Core Fantasy
Deck mastery, adaptation, planning.

Mechanical Identity
Discard
Exhaust
Transform
Generate cards

Typical Traits
Low or no damage
Enables other archetypes
High skill ceiling

Example Effects
Discard for benefit
Pull from discard
Modify card behavior

Hybrid Cards (Intentional Overlaps)
“This card connects systems.”
Hybrid cards intentionally sit between buckets and are high-value design tools.

Common Hybrids
Tempo + Burst (Momentum spenders)
Control + Attrition (Chill + DoT)
Defense + Engine (Guard → retaliation)
Utility + Burst (Discard → nova)

Why Hybrids Matter
Enable creative builds
Reduce linear deckbuilding
Increase replayability
