Monster Modifier ideas:
1. Time-Lagged (Uncommon)

Effect: Enemy’s actions occur 1 turn after declared (delayed actions).
Why it’s cool: Teaches prediction-based play.
Twist: They gain 10% damage for every delayed action queued.

2. Clausebreaker (Rare)

Effect: Every 4th hit against the player gains a random debuff clause: weaken, bleed, vulnerable, or “hand tax” (discard 1).
Why: Fits the Maze theme — enemies penalize violations.

4. Paradox-Bound (Rare)

Effect: Every time you hit this enemy with the same damage type twice in a row, they gain a stacking buff to resist that type.
Why: Encourages alternating combat styles.

5. Fractured Echo (Rare)

Effect: At 50% HP, the enemy spawns a weaker “echo” copy that mirrors their actions with 1-turn delay.
Why: Adds tactical pressure without spamming summons.

6. Temporal Slip (Uncommon)

Effect: 10% chance to completely avoid incoming damage and heal for a small amount instead.
Why: Feels unfair, but numbers can keep it balanced.

7. Contract-Leech (Rare)

Effect: When the player applies a buff to themselves, the enemy gains a temporary buff as well.
Why: Makes buff-heavy builds think twice.

8. Inverted Harmonics (Very rare)

Effect: Damage below a threshold heals the enemy. Damage above it deals 20% more.
Why: Forces burst, discourages chip strategies.

9. Despairmonger (Uncommon)

Effect: First time the enemy drops below 75/50/25% life, it applies a random mental affliction (Fear, Doubt, Frenzy).
Why: Fight evolves over time.

10. Whisper-Wrapped (Rare)

Effect: Every 3 turns, the enemy adds a temporary junk card to the player’s deck.
Why: Deck clogging pressure without overwhelming.

11. Insight Drinker (Uncommon)

Effect: Gains a buff whenever the player draws a card outside of normal draw (loot, scry, discovery).
Why: Counters scry and discovery-heavy builds.

12. Volatile Pulse (Uncommon)

Effect: Upon being hit by critical strikes, releases an AoE pulse dealing lightning damage to player.
Why: High-crit builds must adjust.

13. Icebound Roots (Common)

Effect: Player’s first attack each turn is slowed (costs 10% additional Mana to cast).
Why: Predictable but meaningful.

14. Grudge-Forged (Rare)

Effect: Damage dealt to this enemy increases its damage against you next turn.
Why: Risk/reward pressure.

15. Blood-Tither (Uncommon)

Effect: Steals life equal to 5% of damage taken.
Why: Forces efficient burst.

16. Smoldering Armor (Common)

Effect: Applies a weak Fire damage to attackers.
Why: Low-power, high-flavor.

17. Overclocked Core (Rare)

Effect: Starts combat slow, but gains 2% increased speed and damage every turn.
Why: Builds pressure naturally.

18. Shifting Plates (Uncommon)

Effect: Alternating turns swap between “Armored” (phys resist) and “Evasive” (dodge chance).
Why: Predictable rotation to play around.

19. Scrapstorm (Rare)

Effect: Upon taking damage, has a small chance to retaliate with shards (chip damage).
Why: Good for reinforcing “construct” feel.


20. Wail of the Fallen (Uncommon)

Effect: On death, triggers a “death wail” applying vulnerable for next 2 turn.
Why: Makes killing order matter in packs.

21. Soul-Linked (Rare)

Effect: When two or more Soul-Linked enemies spawn, damage to one is partially shared.
Why: Great for multi-enemy synergy.

22. Echo of Missteps (Uncommon)
Effect: When the player plays the same card twice in a row, the enemy gains a stacking buff.
Why: Promotes deck variety.

23. Rot-Slicked (Common)
Effect: Applies poison on hit.
Why: Bread-and-butter status affix.

24. Threadbare (Uncommon)
Effect: Takes 20% extra damage, but attacks twice at 50% efficiency.
Why: A glass cannon with a twist.

25. Unraveling Aura (Very rare)
Effect: Small chance per turn to “unspool” one temporary buff from player when attacking. (Remove buff)
Why: Thematically perfect for Unraveler spawnlings.

26. Law-Twisted (Rare)

Effect: Randomly changes damage type each turn.
Why: Keeps combat fresh.

27. Hexweaver (Uncommon)
Effect: Every 4 turns, casts a strong curse that lasts 1 turn.
Why: Burst-defense challenge.

28. Bound in Silence (Rare)
Effect: Temporarily silences player Skills every X turns.
Why: Disruptive but predictable.

29. Contract Hoarder (Uncommon)
Effect: Drops double loot but gains defensive stats each time you draw a card.
Why: High risk/high reward fight.

30. Erratic Pulse (Rare)
Effect: At the start of enemy turn, it performs a random action: buff, debuff, or attack. Weighted toward normal attack.
Why: Controlled chaos — perfect Maze flavor.
