### **Occultist** - Master of Curses and Chaos

```
Ascendancy Name: Occultist
Base Class: Witch
Tagline: Master of Curses and Chaos
Theme Color: Purple (#8B00FF)

Description:
"The Occultist specializes in curses, chaos damage, and energy shield manipulation. 
Masters of forbidden knowledge, they corrupt their enemies with powerful hexes while 
protecting themselves with vast reserves of energy shield."

Playstyle Keywords:
- Damage over Time
- Curses
- Energy Shield
- Chaos Damage

Bonuses:
1. [Passive] +20% Chaos Damage
2. [Passive] +50 Maximum Energy Shield
3. [Skill] Curses can affect hexproof enemies
4. [Passive] Energy Shield Recharge Rate increased by 30%
5. [Skill] Apply an additional Curse
6. [Passive] +15% Damage over Time
7. [Skill] Enemies you curse are Maimed
8. [Passive] Cannot be Stunned if Energy Shield is full

Required Level: 15
Unlock Requirement: Complete the Labyrinth
Max Ascendancy Points: 8
```
